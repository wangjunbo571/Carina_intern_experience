Schedule Service

The first part is designed to let people be able to customize the time schedules that they need health care. Consequently, the health care provider can apply the jobs that match their own time schedules

Firstly, people will need to enter "How many hours are you allotted monthly".

Then there is a schedule table. People can click and drag on the table to change the color of elements. It will illustrate the time schedule of the health care seeker

Finally, there is a section to show how many hours have been selected and how many more hours you need to select in order to match your alloted hours

The Second part design is focused on allowing people to match their customized time schedule with the jobs' schedule.

This is in the process that healthcare providers want to apply for a job. In the Jobboard, there will be plenty of Jobcards

In each job card, it will show the matching hours of this job with your schedule.

For each day, one corresponding blue circle will show the matching hours. If it is no matching hours, it will be grey

When users hover over the schedule circles, it will show the details of matching hours in the form of schedule table
