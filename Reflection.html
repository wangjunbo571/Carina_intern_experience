Reflection

Carina is a start-up healthcare company, which located at the Seattle Downtown. They connect qualified home care aides with Medicaid and state funded clients in Washington State, through providing an online platform. Clients will post their health care needs with relative information. The verified health care provider will then apply the jobs, that they are interested. The health care managers will coordinate the contracts.

My works start with evaluating the website and interactive with the users of Carina. I worked with the UX designers in the Carina to experience this website as a user and did some phone surveys to collect the opinions from the real Carina IPs, in order to generate the idea about what services should be provided and what kind of current features need to be improved. In this step, the information collection skills and knowledge will be used.

Then I implemented those designs into real use, through React, HTML, JavaSpring, SQL, python and so forth. In my intern experience, four projects were completed, including scheduling service, matching service, SMS service and UX cleanup design.
