SMS Service

The first part design is focused on the statistic SMS service, through twillio and api.ai

The Carina will send out prompt messages to users to check their availability, based on their attributes. Hello {first name}, clients appreciate providers that update their Carina profiles. Text “1” if you are available to care for a new client or “2” if you are not. Text “NO” to unsubscribe.

And the carina can send back their corresponding texts, based on user's returned availability status. "Thanks for updating your profile! Clients will see you listed as {available/ unavailable}. Login to edit at any time: www.cariancare.com/profile/edit."

And they will also make respondence messgaes to the invaild input

The second part is focusing on touching the database and setup cron to make follow up actions

Several new columns, callled last updated, last receieved, last sent, were created in order to facilitate the conditional judgement

The promption messgaes will be sent out periodicly based on the cron service

And the cron service will also help to check whether user responded in time. If not they will send out follow up messages or set the status to unavailable directly
