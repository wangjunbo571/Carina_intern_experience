The first part of this design is to have a matching service. From the form on the left, job applicants can chose what kind of jobs they want by answering the eight questions. They help IPs to discribe their jobs criteria

Then jobcards will show whether this jobcard match that criteria by cross or tick. The unimportant area, which are not chosen by users, will be hidden.

When people do not chose any filter criteria, jobcards will show all the information. Users can more easily find the matching health care providers in Carina
